About Our College

    Government Polytechnic Mudhol was established to provide high-quality technical education to students in Karnataka. With a focus on both theoretical knowledge and hands-on skills, GPT Mudhol is dedicated to producing industry-ready engineers and technicians.


    Government Polytechnic, Mudhol (GPT Mudhol) is a well-regarded technical education institute located in Mudhol, Bagalkot district, Karnataka. Established in 2009, it operates under the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Karnataka and is approved by AICTE

    Courses Offered GPT Mudhol provides four full-time diploma programs, each with a duration of three years: - Civil Engineering - Computer Science and Engineering - Electronics and Communication Engineering - Mechanical Engineering Each course typically has 54 seats, totaling an intake capacity of 216 students
